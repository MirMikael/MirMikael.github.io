Title: Adaptive Sliding Mode Control for Quadrotor UAVs under Disturbances Using Multi-Layer Perceptron (2024)
Authors: Mir Mikael Fatemi, Prof.Dr. Adel Akbarimajd
Impact: This article introduces a new, robust control method for quadcopter drones that uses a neural network to adapt and improve traditional control. This approach leads to significantly reduced vibrations and better performance, including lower energy consumption. Ultimately, the research shows that combining AI and traditional control results in more resilient and efficient drones.

Authors: Mir Mikael Fatemi, Prof.Dr. Adel Akbarimajd
