STM32F107RCT Header Board

This marked the inception of my journey in designing and constructing ARM-based circuits. The primary aim was educational, as well as for initial testing of the codes in my projects. The process of building this circuit significantly enhanced my proficiency in Altium software and deepened my understanding of design concepts. Moreover, the board has consistently served as one of my pivotal work tools, especially in conjunction with STM32 microcontrollers.
The design of this board is centered on the F107RCT, encompassing all microcontroller pins, excluding specialized ones like restart and power, other pins are as I/O. This feature renders it exceptionally versatile for testing a wide array of codes.
